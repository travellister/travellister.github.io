About Travellister


When packing for an overseas trip, most travellers would tap on their past knowledge and experiences, or even seek the advice of their friends and family members, who have already travelled to those places before.


However, the problem arises when travellers are venturing to places that are less travelled. Instead of spending a huge amount of time reading up on travel blogs or doing online research, we realised that it would be of much help to generate a packing list that is specially customised based on the intended travel itinerary. Besides saving time, travellers also avert the undesirable possibility of over or under-packing for the trip, thereby making their travel experience as hassle-free as possible.


Hence, we aim to provide users like yourself with a packing list that is generated according to factors such as the place of travel, duration of travel and types of activities that you intend to engage in.


On top of that, there are many more additional features such as the to-do list and the currency rate converter which are available on the website. With that, we aim to implement this website as a one-stop holistic application for you to obtain all the information and web links necessary for your travel plans.
